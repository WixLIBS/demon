Our History

The New York Seafood Journey

Founded in 2007, New York Seafood Market was born from a passion for seafood cuisine. Our founder, Mrs Lynn, spent over 7 years perfecting her craft in some seafood establishments before bringing these exceptional flavors to St. Petersburg.

What started as a small take-out counter and market has blossomed into one of St. Petersburg's community favorites, beloved by locals and visitors alike, we've maintained our commitment to quality and freshness.

Today, we continue to serve our community with pride, offering a dining experience that combines the bold flavors of Seafood with the warm hospitality of Florida's Gulf Coast.

Our Journey

How We've Grown

2007

The Beginning

New York Seafood Restaurant originated as a small take out counter & market on 34th street, bringing tasty seafood and fresh seafood to St. Petersburg tables.

2016

Community Focus

During challenging times, we pivoted to support our community with affordable family meals and donations to essential workers.

2022

Culinary Innovation

Continuing to innovate our menu while remaining true to our roots and the authentic flavors that made us who we are.

2024

Community Milestone

Reached over 300 positive reviews across platforms, with customers praising our commitment to quality and authentic flavors.

2025

Expansion

Growing demand led to our expansion, adding a full-service dining room to enhance the customer experience.
